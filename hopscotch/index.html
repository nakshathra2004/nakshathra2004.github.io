How to Play:

1. Click "Throw Stone" to place your stone on the current round number

2. Start from the brown START tile and hop UP from 1 to 10

3. Skip the stone tile (purple) on your way up

4. Once you reach 10, hop back DOWN to the START tile

5. Complete all 10 rounds (stone on each number 1-10) to win!

Note: The highlighted tile (orange) shows where to hop next
